Chosen Collective Consulting, LLC is an education and community centered consulting firm that supports organizations in shifting towards a climate of equity, justice and inclusion while intentionally centering care and support for BIPOC and/or LGBTQIA+ folks. We promote the value and knowledge fostered by lived experiences that are often marginalized or structurally excluded in organizational environments. Chosen also hosts yearly, “Reset-Retreats", a travel retreat space for folks of color, and/or LGBTQIA+ folks who do community care-centered and organizing work.
