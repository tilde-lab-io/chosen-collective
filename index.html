Our Vision

We believe in a world where BIPOC and/or LGBTQIA+ folks no longer have to "rest" or "retreat" from oppressive systems and cultures. We work towards this vision by partnering with organizations to create equitable, just, inclusive, and supportive environments that empower BIPOC and LGBTQIA+ people to thrive, lead, advocate, and thoughtfully engage

Our Values

Community

"We are each other's keepers". The only way to enact justice is through communal connections that support, challenge, and nourish us

Wellness

Caring and centering our individual and collective social, emotional, physical, and spiritual wellness is imperative to sustainable social justice movement building

Education

Learning about the past and how it informs present contexts is important to identifying and making meaningful change. Education must include tools that support critical dialogue that leads to individual and collective growth

Equity

Remedying historical truths and disadvantages by actionably addressing contemporary contexts and needs of communities directly impacted by racial, gender, religious, LGBTQIA+, xenophobic, and other forms of systemic socio-economic inequality

Action

Has always "spoken louder than words". Any plan to support social justice and equity must be clear, tangible, measurable, and center the communities it is meant to impact

Our Mission

By leveraging our expertise, we help organizations foster cultures of inclusivity that drive sustainable growth while also creating positive impact for historically oppressed and disinvested communities. The work of equity and inclusion is a shared responsibility, and we're committed to working alongside individuals and organizations to make this vision a reality
